Our Story

Where every cup tells a story

Nestled on Akureyri's beloved main shopping street, Bláa Kannan — "The Blue Jug" — stands as an architectural gem and a cornerstone of the town's café culture. With its quirky, eclectic interior and warm atmosphere, it has become the gathering place for locals and travellers alike.

Everything we serve is crafted with care. Our cakes, pastries, and breads are baked fresh daily in our own bakery, using time-honoured recipes and the finest Icelandic ingredients. Our coffee is roasted to perfection — rich, aromatic, and always served with a smile.
